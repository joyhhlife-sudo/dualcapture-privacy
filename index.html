This Privacy Policy explains how X Dual Capture handles information when you use the app. X Dual Capture is designed as an on-device camera tool that allows supported iPhone devices to capture content from the front and rear cameras. Device Permissions X Dual Capture may request the following system permissions. Camera Camera access is required to show camera previews and capture photos or videos using the available front and rear cameras. Microphone Microphone access is used only when you record video with audio enabled. You may deny microphone access and use supported features that do not require audio. Photo Library Photo Library permission may be requested when you choose to save captured photos or videos to your library. Depending on the iOS version and your permission selection, the app may receive permission only to add new media or to access media you specifically select. 5. Permission Control You can review or change permissions at any time in: Settings > Privacy & Security or: Settings > X Dual Capture Disabling a permission may prevent the related feature from working, but it does not prevent you from using other available features. 6. In-App Purchases X Dual Capture may offer an optional one-time Pro purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. X Tools does not receive or store your full payment card number, bank details, or Apple Account password. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking X Dual Capture does not use third-party advertising SDKs. X Dual Capture does not display personalized advertisements. X Dual Capture does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies. X Tools does not use your camera content for advertising, profiling, facial recognition databases, or artificial intelligence model training. Apple may independently process App Store, purchase, device, diagnostic, or usage information according to Apple's own terms and privacy policies. 8. Local App Data The app may store limited settings locally on your device, including: • Selected camera layout • Recording preferences • Zoom or camera settings • Permission-related status • Free usage status • Pro entitlement status • Other interface preferences This information is used only to operate the app and provide your selected settings. Deleting the app may remove locally stored settings. Photos and videos already saved to your Photo Library are managed separately by iOS and are not automatically deleted when you remove the app. 9. Data Retention Because X Tools does not upload your camera content or personal information to an X Tools server, X Tools does not retain such content on its servers. Media saved to your Photo Library remains there until you delete or manage it through the Photos app or another connected service. Apple may retain purchase and transaction records according to its own legal and operational requirements. 10. Data Sharing X Tools does not sell your personal information. You are responsible for protecting your device, Apple Account, backups, and any media you choose to share or upload. 12. Children's Privacy X Dual Capture is a general-purpose camera utility and is not specifically directed to children.
