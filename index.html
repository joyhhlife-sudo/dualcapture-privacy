1. Overview

X Tools respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how X Dual Capture handles information when you use the app.

X Dual Capture is an on-device camera utility for supported iPhone models. It can capture front and rear camera views, create Picture-in-Picture or split layouts, and save photos or videos. No X Tools account is required.

Unless this policy says otherwise, X Tools does not collect, upload, store, sell, rent, or share your photos, videos, audio recordings, live camera content, or personal information.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

X Dual Capture does not collect:

  • Photos, videos, live camera frames, or microphone recordings
  • Face data, biometric identifiers, or facial templates
  • Precise or approximate location
  • Contacts, email addresses, phone numbers, or account credentials
  • Advertising identifiers, browsing history, or search history
  • Payment card or bank account information

The app does not require registration and does not create a user profile on X Tools servers.

3. On-device Camera and Media Processing

Camera preview, dual-camera capture, layout composition, zoom processing, recording, and export are performed locally on your device.

X Tools does not operate a server that receives or stores your camera media. Your content remains under your control unless you choose to save it to Photos, share it, transfer it, or back it up through Apple or another service.

If you use the iOS share sheet, the destination app or service may process the selected content under its own privacy policy.

4. Device Permissions

Camera

Camera access is required to show previews and capture photos or videos from supported front and rear cameras.

Microphone

Microphone access is used only when recording video with audio enabled. You may deny microphone access and continue using features that do not require sound.

Photo Library

Photo Library access may be requested when you save captured media. Depending on iOS and your choice, the app may receive add-only access or access limited to selected items.

Managing permissions

You can review or change access in Settings › Privacy & Security or Settings › X Dual Capture. Disabling a permission may prevent the related feature from working.

5. In-App Purchases

X Dual Capture may offer an optional one-time Pro purchase. Apple processes App Store purchases. X Tools does not receive or store your full payment card number, bank information, or Apple Account password.

The app may receive limited transaction information from Apple, such as the product identifier, purchase completion status, entitlement status, and information needed to restore an eligible purchase. This information is used only to provide or restore purchased features.

6. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

  • No third-party advertising SDKs are used.
  • No personalized advertisements are displayed.
  • X Dual Capture does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.
  • Your camera content is not used for advertising, profiling, facial-recognition databases, or AI model training.

Apple may independently process App Store, purchase, device, diagnostic, or usage information according to Apple’s own terms and privacy policies.

7. Data Stored Locally

The app may store limited information on your device so that it can operate and remember your preferences, including:

  • Selected layout, camera, zoom, and recording preferences
  • Permission-related status and interface preferences
  • Free-use counters or limit status
  • Pro entitlement status or purchase restoration state

Deleting the app may remove locally stored settings. Media already saved in Photos is managed separately by iOS and is not automatically deleted when the app is removed.

8. Data Sharing and Retention

X Tools does not sell personal information and does not share your photos, videos, audio recordings, or live camera content with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing companies.

Because X Tools does not upload your camera media to its servers, it does not retain that media. Files saved to Photos remain there until you manage or delete them. Apple may retain App Store transaction records according to its own requirements.

Information may be disclosed where required by valid law or legal process. In ordinary circumstances, X Tools has no access to media stored only on your device.

9. Security and Children’s Privacy

Local processing is designed to reduce privacy risk. However, no device, operating system, storage system, backup, or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure. You are responsible for protecting your device, Apple Account, backups, and shared media.

X Dual Capture is a general-purpose camera utility and is not specifically directed to children. X Tools does not knowingly collect children’s personal information through the app. Parents and guardians should supervise the use of camera, microphone, sharing, and online services where appropriate.

10. Apple and Third-Party Services

The app relies on Apple technologies and services, which may include iOS, camera and microphone frameworks, Photos, App Store, In-App Purchase, iCloud or device backup, and the iOS share sheet.

These services are operated by Apple and are governed by Apple’s terms and privacy policies. When you export or share media to another app or service, that third party’s terms and privacy policy apply.

11. Your Choices and Rights

You can manage information related to the app by:

  • Changing camera, microphone, and Photos permissions
  • Deleting photos or videos in the Photos app
  • Deleting X Dual Capture to remove its local settings
  • Managing purchases through your Apple Account
  • Contacting X Tools with a privacy question

Because X Tools does not maintain user accounts or store your camera media on its servers, it generally cannot access, retrieve, correct, export, or delete media stored only on your device.

Your rights may vary by country or region. This policy does not limit rights that cannot legally be excluded.

12. Updates and Contact

We may update this policy when the app, law, or operating practices change. The revised version will be posted here with an updated date. Material changes may also be communicated in the app or App Store listing where appropriate.

一、概述

X Tools 尊重并重视你的隐私。本隐私政策说明你在使用 X 同步双拍 时，App 如何处理相关信息。

X 同步双拍是一款在设备本地运行的相机工具，可在受支持的 iPhone 上同时记录前置和后置摄像头画面，生成画中画、分屏等布局并保存照片或视频。使用本 App 无需注册 X Tools 账号。

除本政策另有明确说明外，X Tools 不会收集、上传、存储、出售、出租或分享你的照片、视频、录音、实时摄像头内容或个人信息。

二、我们不收集的数据

X 同步双拍不收集：

  • 照片、视频、实时摄像头画面或麦克风录音
  • 人脸数据、生物识别标识或人脸模板
  • 精确位置或大致位置
  • 通讯录、邮箱、电话号码或账号凭据
  • 广告标识符、浏览历史或搜索历史
  • 银行卡或银行账户信息

本 App 不要求注册，也不会在 X Tools 服务器上建立用户画像。

三、设备本地处理

相机预览、双摄拍摄、布局合成、变焦处理、录像和导出均在你的设备本地完成。

X Tools 不运营用于接收或存储用户摄像头媒体的服务器。除非你主动保存到“照片”、分享、传输或通过 Apple 或其他服务备份，否则内容始终由你控制。

当你使用 iOS 系统分享面板时，目标 App 或服务可能依据其自身隐私政策处理你选择的内容。

四、设备权限

相机

相机权限用于显示预览，并使用设备支持的前置和后置摄像头拍摄照片或视频。

麦克风

麦克风权限仅在启用声音并录制视频时使用。你可以拒绝麦克风权限，并继续使用不依赖声音的功能。

照片图库

保存拍摄内容时，App 可能申请照片图库权限。根据 iOS 版本及你的选择，App 可能仅获得添加照片的权限，或只能访问你明确选择的内容。

权限管理

你可以在 设置 › 隐私与安全性 或 设置 › X 同步双拍 中修改权限。关闭权限可能导致对应功能无法使用。

五、App 内购买

X 同步双拍可能提供可选的一次性 Pro 买断。购买由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。X Tools 不会接收或存储你的完整银行卡号、银行信息或 Apple 账户密码。

App 可能从 Apple 获得有限的交易信息，例如产品标识符、购买完成状态、权益状态，以及恢复符合条件的购买所需的信息。这些信息仅用于提供或恢复已购买功能。

六、分析、广告与跟踪

  • 不使用第三方广告 SDK。
  • 不展示个性化广告。
  • 不会跨越其他公司的 App 或网站跟踪你。
  • 不会将摄像头内容用于广告、用户画像、人脸识别数据库或 AI 模型训练。

Apple 可能依据其自身条款和隐私政策，独立处理 App Store、购买、设备、诊断或使用情况信息。

七、本地保存的数据

为了运行 App 并保留偏好设置，App 可能在你的设备上保存有限信息，包括：

  • 已选择的布局、摄像头、变焦和录像偏好
  • 与权限有关的状态及界面偏好
  • 免费使用次数或限制状态
  • Pro 权益状态或购买恢复状态

删除 App 可能移除这些本地设置。已经保存到“照片”中的媒体由 iOS 单独管理，不会因为删除 App 而自动删除。

八、数据分享与保留

X Tools 不出售个人信息，也不会将你的照片、视频、录音或实时摄像头内容分享给广告商、数据经纪商或营销机构。

由于 X Tools 不会把摄像头媒体上传至服务器，因此不会在服务器上保留这些内容。保存到“照片”的文件会保留至你自行管理或删除。Apple 可能依据自身要求保留 App Store 交易记录。

仅在有效法律或法律程序要求时，相关信息才可能依法披露。在通常情况下，X Tools 无法访问仅存储在你设备上的媒体。

X 同步双拍是一款通用相机工具，并非专门面向儿童。X Tools 不会通过本 App 有意收集儿童个人信息。家长或监护人应在适当情况下监督儿童使用相机、麦克风、分享和网络服务。
